How to read this: when two symbols are negatively correlated, buying both at the same time does not produce a hedge where both win — by definition, when one rises the other tends to fall. What this app does instead is identify the pair with the strongest live negative correlation, then propose a primary leg (the one showing the stronger reversal/momentum setup) with a larger position and wider target, and a hedge leg (the negatively-correlated symbol) sized smaller with a tighter stop — so if the primary leg works, its gain is structured to outweigh a likely small loss on the hedge leg. This is a risk-asymmetry technique, not a market-neutral hedge, and correlations can break down or flip without warning. Position sizing, stop placement, and the decision to trade are entirely yours. This tool provides information only and is not financial advice.
